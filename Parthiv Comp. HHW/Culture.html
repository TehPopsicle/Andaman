Culture In the Andaman

Where Traditions Blossom in Tropical Harmony!

Traditional Music and Dance

The indigenous tribes have their distinct music and dance forms, which are integral to their festivals and rituals.

Craftsmanship

Local artisans are known for their skills in woodwork, shell crafts, and basketry.

Festivals

The islands celebrate various festivals, such as the Island Tourism Festival, which showcases the cultural diversity of the region.

Cuisine

The local cuisine is a mix of Indian and Southeast Asian flavors, with an abundance of seafood dishes.
